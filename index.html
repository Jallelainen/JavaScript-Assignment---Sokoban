Sokoban

How to play

The objective of the Sokoban game is to move boxes to designated locations by pushing them.

These boxes are located inside a room surrounded by walls.

The pusher can move up, down, left and right, but cannot pass through walls or boxes, and can only push one box at a time (never pull).

At any time, a square can only be occupied by either a wall, a box, or the pusher.

How to move

Use the arrow keys to move in the direction you want.
